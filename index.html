About

I turn thoughtful design into fast, polished web experiences.

I am a front-end developer based in Regina, Saskatchewan, combining a technical foundation in Computer Engineering with specialized training in Web Development from St. Clair College. My interest in development started with the simple idea that a page can shape how people understand, trust, and connect with a brand.

Today, I focus on building interfaces that do more than look good. I care about clarity, usability, speed, and conversion, because strong front-end work should help businesses communicate better, earn confidence faster, and move users toward meaningful action.
