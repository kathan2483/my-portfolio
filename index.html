The Mindset

I lose sleep over mediocre work. I refine until it's right.

Based in Regina, Saskatchewan, I bring a deeply analytical approach to web design. I completed a 3-year diploma in Computer Engineering followed by a 2-year diploma in Web Development from St. Clair College. This dual foundation means I don't just understand how a page looks—I understand how the underlying architecture functions, performs, and scales.

When I build a website, my priority is the business objective. I care about clean semantic structure, lightning-fast load times, and intuitive user experiences. I take absolute ownership of the process from local deployment via Git to final launch, ensuring the end product is built to increase conversion rates, not just chase trendy animations.
